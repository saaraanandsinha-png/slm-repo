# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: June 2026**

## Overview

Aloof is designed with privacy at its core. The app runs entirely on your device — no data is ever collected, stored externally, or shared with anyone.

## What we access

Aloof requests **notification access** to read incoming WhatsApp messages. This is the only permission required.

## What we do with your data

- Messages are read **locally on your device** to extract reminders
- Nothing is sent to any server, cloud service, or third party
- No analytics, no tracking, no ads
- Reminders are stored in a local database on your device only

## What we don't do

- We do not collect personal information
- We do not share any data with third parties
- We do not store message content beyond what is saved as a reminder on your device
- We do not have access to your data — ever

## On-device AI

Aloof uses an on-device language model (FunctionGemma 270M) to process messages. All inference runs locally on your device. The model has no network access and cannot transmit data.

## Data deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete it at any time by clearing the app's storage or uninstalling the app.
